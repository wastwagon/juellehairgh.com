## üé® Design Specifications

### **Layout:**
- **Desktop:** 4 columns grid (`grid-cols-4`)
- **Tablet:** 2 columns grid (`md:grid-cols-2`)
- **Mobile:** 1 column (`grid-cols-1`)

### **Card Design:**
- **Size:** Aspect ratio 16:9 or 4:3 (rectangular)
- **Style:** Modern cards with:
  - Large featured image (background or top)
  - Overlay gradient for text readability
  - White text with shadow
  - Icon in top-left corner
  - CTA button at bottom
  - Hover effects (scale, shadow)

### **Image Requirements:**
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Size:** Minimum 800x600px (recommended 1200x900px)
- **Style:** High-quality product photos or lifestyle images
- **Content:** Should showcase the category/product clearly
