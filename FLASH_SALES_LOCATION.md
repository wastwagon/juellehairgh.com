## What You Can Do in Flash Sales:

1. **Create New Flash Sale:**
   - Click "New Flash Sale" button
   - Set title, description, dates, discount %
   - Add products to the sale
   - Toggle "isActive" to enable/disable

2. **Edit Existing Flash Sale:**
   - Click "Edit" on any flash sale
   - Update details and products
   - Save changes

3. **Delete Flash Sale:**
   - Click "Delete" button
   - Confirm deletion

4. **View Active Status:**
   - Green badge = Currently active
   - Red badge = Not active or expired
